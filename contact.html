FAQ

Common questions

What is tennis sparring?
Tennis sparring is a structured hitting session with an experienced partner. Unlike coaching, the focus is on gameplay, rallying, and match practice, helping you apply what you've learned in a real game environment.
Do I need to be a certain level to join?
Not at all. We welcome players of all levels. From beginners looking to build confidence to advanced players wanting high-intensity match practice, our sparrers adapt to your level.
How is sparring different from coaching?
Coaching focuses on technique through drills and instruction. Sparring focuses on putting those skills into practice through real gameplay. Both are valuable, with sparring being the perfect complement to your coaching sessions, but at a fraction of the cost.
Where are the sessions held?
Sessions are held at public courts across Singapore. Court location will be discussed and confirmed before you book your session with us. We aim to arrange for sessions to be held at courts most convenient to you.
How do I book a session?
Simply reach out via WhatsApp or email with your preferred schedule, your level, and what kind of session you're looking for. We'll match you with the right sparring partner and confirm your slot.
Which package should I choose?
If you're new or just want to try it out, the 1-session ad hoc option is perfect. If you have a specific area to improve — like mechanics, strategy, or a particular shot — the 6-session package is a great fit. For players committed to consistent growth over time, the 12-session package delivers the best results.
